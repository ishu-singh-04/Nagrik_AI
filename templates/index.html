BACKEND ARCHITECTURE

Automated AI Governance Pipeline

How Gemini 3.5 Flash processes, filters, and routes civic issues in real-time.

AI Auto-Classification

Powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash

Instantly scans the uploaded image to auto-detect the issue type (Pothole, Waste, Water Leakage) without any manual tagging.

AI Context & Smart Merge

Bilingual Auto-Summarization

Generates automated summaries in English & Hindi. Merges overlapping local reports into a single ticket with an active count to stop spam.

Department Isolation

Role-Based Access Guard

Enforces zero cross-department data pollution. PWD officers only stream potholes, while Jal Board views strictly water utilities.

Dynamic ETA Allocator

Rule-Based Deadline Setup

Automatically calculates SLA resolution clocks based on danger metrics (e.g., hanging wires get 24h, water leaks get 12h).

Dual-Image Resolution Audit

Computer-Vision Verification

Compares the officer's 'After' image against the citizen's 'Before' image using multi-modal AI to calculate an automated confidence score.

Geo-Spatial Telemetry

Reverse-Geocoded Reporting

Locks live GPS coordinates, mapping them instantly to state, city, and pincode tiers to power the localized administration dashboard.
